Multiple Code Lines

Minecraft's line count varies greatly by version (Java/Bedrock) and what's counted
(core, mods, decompiled vs. original), but estimates range from hundreds of thousands
to over a million, with one decompilation suggesting around 600,000 lines, while some
sources claim closer to 1.8 million lines for the whole system, showing its immense and
ever-growing codebase
