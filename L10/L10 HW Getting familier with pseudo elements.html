ABOUT ROBLOX'S "Builderman's club"

  • Builders Club
  • Turbo Builders Club
  • Outrageous Builders Club

The "Builders Club" was a former premium membership program on the gaming platform Roblox that
has since been replaced by "Roblox Premium". The program had three distinct categories, also
referred to as tiers, each offering increasing benefits:

Each tier also granted users a specific colored "Hard Hat" virtual item corresponding to their
membership level, which became a symbol of a player's veteran status on the platform. The Builders
Club program was discontinued in 2019 and replaced with the current subscription model known as Roblox Premium.
